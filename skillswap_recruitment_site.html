🚀 Join the SkillSwap Team

SkillSwap is a platform where you teach what you know and learn what you don’t — no money, just time. A barter system for learning.

🔁 What’s the Big Idea?

Imagine teaching someone graphic design and earning credit to learn public speaking. That's SkillSwap. We enable peer-to-peer learning that’s simple, trust-based, and free.

🎯 Mission

To democratize education by creating a skill-exchange platform that empowers everyone to be a teacher and a learner.
